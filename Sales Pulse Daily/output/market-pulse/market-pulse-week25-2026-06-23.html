📊 Franchise Lead Time Analysis (Market Temperature)

What it shows: Factory lead times from franchise distributors (Arrow, Avnet, Mouser, Digi-Key, Newark, Future, TTI, Master)
Comparison: Current 30-day average vs 90-day baseline
Status Classification:
• 🟢 Stable — Lead time within ±10% of baseline (normal market)
• 🟡 Constrained — 10-50% increase from baseline (supply tightening)
• 🔴 Allocated — >50% increase or 26+ weeks (allocation active or imminent)
• 🟢↓ Recovery — Lead times declining from previous period (market loosening)
Distribution Buckets: 0-4w (Stock/Short), 5-12w (Normal), 13-20w (Extended), 21+w (Allocated)
Why it matters: Franchise lead times = factory lead times = earliest signal of manufacturer constraints
