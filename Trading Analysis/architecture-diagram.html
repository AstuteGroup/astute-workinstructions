Pricing Rule (when franchise has stock)

Our price ≈ 20-30% of franchise best price
Why: older DC, untraceable stock. Buyer (broker) needs margin room to resell vs franchise.
<$1 → 20%  |  $1-10 → 25%  |  $10+ → 30%
Broker sales can override if market clears differently.
